Love to Run

I am Christopher Hillman and I love to run..
Running is what gives me the strength to get through the day, recently I have had an injury with two herniated discs in my spine and it is the worst thing ever. With a little luck i will return to running in around 8 weeks, just after England win the World Cup. Im making this website as I need a running related outlet.
Once I am able to run again I intend to run a sub 3 hour Marathon and even if it breaks my back I intend to succeed.
